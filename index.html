As a new student at Epicodus, I'd like for you to know a bit more about me

I'm one of a few of us that bikes in daily and I'm rarely without a coffee. This week has been challenging; usually in a good way and hopefully this excercise gives me a good idea of my progress and what aspects of the coursework I need to imrove upon.

Background

Originally from the midwest, I attended school in NYC and fell into a position as a frontend developer for a few agencies during the .com boom. With the inevitable .com bust and general unlivability of NYC immediately following 9/11, I left NYC and spent the next few years focusing on music as a touring musician and a label owner. With the advent of streaming and that industry essentially going belly up, I tried my hand a few different things before following my wife, Coco, to Oregon for her to further her career in the nursing field.

Now that I'm here and we're settled, I have to opportunity to learn how to code properly and shift my means of supporting myself from job to career. Despite the fact that I am woefully out of practice, I'm enjoying the program and the challenges of immersing myself into re-learning (hopefully properly this time) the skills necessary to pursue coding and programming as a career.
